NAACL 2015 Publications

This page is maintained by Matt Post and Adam Lopez, the NAACL 2015 publications co-chairs. Our job is to make sure the conference proceedings get put together well and on time.

When the time is nearer, this page will contain information for two groups of people: (1) researchers submitting papers (first for peer review, and later the camera ready versions of accepted papers), and (2) organizers of co-hosted conferences and workshops (to be determined).

If you have any questions or concerns, you can reach us by email at naacl-pub-chairs@googlegroups.com.

Information for paper writers

Camera-ready copies were due on March 20. LaTeX (recommended) and Microsoft Word template files are available from the following links.

Please view the camera-ready FAQ for important information about submitting your paper. Please skim this even if you are an old pro at submitting papers, since small things change every year!

Information for workshop organizers

The main obligation of workshop organizers (including the Student Research Workshop and Demonstrations) to the publications chairs is to deliver a camera-ready "book" of the workshop proceedings on time (by April 15, 2015). Our job is to then bundle the workshop books together with the main proceedings and release the complete proceedings for use at the conference and for hosting on the ACL Anthology.

Information about this can be found in the Book page.
